| DO’s | DON’Ts |
|------|--------|
| Verify information before sharing it. Cross-check news or claims with at least two reliable sources. | Don’t post in anger or frustration. Wait, reflect, and reconsider before posting. |
| Engage respectfully in comment sections. Disagree with ideas, not people. Treat others as you would in real life. | Don’t accept follow requests from suspicious or bot-like accounts. They may spread misinformation. |
| Review and clean up old posts periodically. Past posts may not reflect who you are now. | Don’t publicly post your travel plans or announce you are away from home. It is a safety risk. |
| Highlight your academic achievements, projects, or certifications. | Don’t use social media to vent about professors, classmates, or institutions. It may have serious consequences. |
| Use social media constructively for networking (connect with seniors, alumni, mentors). | Don’t engage in or ignore cyberbullying. Speak up or report it instead of scrolling past. |
| Turn off non-essential notifications during study hours to maintain focus. | Don’t click on suspicious links or giveaway posts. They are often phishing attempts. |
| Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA on all accounts. | Don’t use weak or repeated passwords. Use a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters. |
